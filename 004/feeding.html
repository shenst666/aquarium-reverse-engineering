      Beyond the schedule

      五个每个鱼友都吃过亏才学会的法则。

      少喂几乎总是更好

      新手缸里鱼类死亡的最常见原因就是过度喂食。食物残渣腐烂分解，氨浓度飙升，氮循环崩溃。拿不准时，少喂。

      菜单要多样化

      没有一种食物能覆盖所有营养需求。在 2-3 个颗粒/薄片品牌间轮换，每周补充 1-2 次冷冻食物。

      食物要匹配进食区域

      上层鱼（斗鱼、曼龙）需要浮性食物。中层鱼（灯鱼）需要缓沉型。底层鱼（鼠鱼、异型）需要能沉到底的颗粒。

      60 秒法则

      无论喂什么，应在约 60 秒内被吃完。90 秒后还漂着的食物就是太多了——捞出来，下次少喂。

      偶尔停食

      每周停食一天对大多数鱼种是正常做法。这模拟了自然环境，让消化系统休息——对迷鳃鱼和金鱼尤其有益。

      让鱼缸能抗假期

      健康鱼在已循环的缸中可以 5-7 天不喂食。自动喂食器方便但不可靠——超过一周的旅行，请人只检查水质，不要喂食。

      完整鱼类喂食指南

      28 种观赏鱼：喂多少、多久喂一次、喂什么。

      喂鱼看起来很简单——丢颗粒进去，鱼吃了，完事。实际上它是新手缸中可预防性鱼类死亡的首要原因，因为正确的量和频率因品种差异巨大。一条斗鱼每天需要 6-8 颗分两顿；一条海水海金鱼需要 3-4 次少量；一条异型几乎只需要藻类和沉木。搞错喂食计划，你会看到两种结果之一：腹胀便秘的鱼出现鳔病，或者饿得褪色不长。

      鱼应该多久喂一次？按品种汇总

      健康成年热带鱼的默认方案是 每天两次, 每次以 60 秒内吃完为量。以下是主要例外：

      • Bettas: 2× daily, 3–4 pellets each meal, one fast day per week.
      • Goldfish (fancy): 2× daily, sinking pellets only, weekly blanched pea day.
      • Corydoras & bottom dwellers: 1× daily, sinking food after lights-out so other fish don't steal it.
      • Anthias (marine): 3–4× daily — they're adapted to constant plankton grazing on reefs.
      • Tangs & herbivores: Nori sheet clipped in the tank for constant grazing, plus 1–2 pellet feedings.
      • Plecos & algae-eaters: 1× daily evening feed of algae wafers; supplement with blanched courgette weekly.
      • Shrimp & snails: Tiny pinch every 2–3 days. They graze biofilm constantly — 过度喂食 kills them faster than starvation.
      • Juveniles & fry (all species): 3–5× daily smaller meals for steady growth.

      60 秒法则: how much to feed

      Regardless of species, the universal portion-size guide is the 60 秒法则: feed only what your fish consume in about a minute. Food still floating after 90 seconds is 过度喂食. Net it out (it'll otherwise decay into ammonia) and reduce the next meal by 20%.

      这条规则胜过所有"每条鱼 X 颗"的公式，因为它自动适应：

      • Fish appetite (lower in cold months, higher when breeding)
      • Water temperature (warmer water = faster metabolism = more food required)
      • Group size (more fish = more competition = faster consumption)
      • Activity level (post-water-change fish eat more enthusiastically)

      薄片 vs 颗粒 vs 冷冻 vs 活饵：哪种鱼食最好？

      健康的饮食应在不同食物类型间轮换。每种都有其用途：

      常见喂食错误及如何避免

      1. Overfeeding. If your water is cloudy, your algae is blooming, or your nitrate climbs faster than expected — you're 过度喂食. Halve your portions for two weeks.
      2. One food only. Even the best flake doesn't cover every nutritional need. Rotate at least two types of food, and supplement with frozen 1–2× per week.
      3. Wrong food zone. Surface flakes for bottom-dwelling corys means they starve. Sinking pellets for surface bettas means they ignore the food. Match food behaviour to fish behaviour.
      4. Tropical food for goldfish. Wrong nutrient balance. Goldfish need goldfish-specific food — they're cyprinids, not characins.
      5. Feeder fish for predators. Live feeder goldfish carry disease and contain thiaminase, which damages predatory fish liver over time. Use frozen or pellet food instead.
      6. Never fasting. Most fish benefit from one day a week without food. Bettas, goldfish, and gouramis especially — it prevents constipation and lets their digestive systems reset.

      假期喂食：你不在时怎么办

      健康成年鱼在已循环的缸中可以舒适地度过 5-7 天不进食。它们是冷血动物，代谢缓慢。长途旅行有三种选择：

      • Up to 1 week: Do a 30% water change before leaving, then leave the tank entirely. Skip food. The fish will be fine.
      • 1–2 weeks: Hire a sitter, but instruct them to do nothing but observe and check the heater. No feeding. Overfeeding by a well-meaning friend kills more holiday fish than starvation.
      • Longer trips: Auto-feeders work but are unreliable. Better: a sitter trained on a pre-portioned daily packet (one envelope per day), with strict instructions to feed exactly one and no more.

      喂多少、喂多久：热门品种详解

      以上是快速版本。对于被问到最多的品种，这里是更详细的答案：

      • How much to feed a betta fish: 3–4 pellets, twice daily, with one fast day weekly. See our full betta feeding guide for portion sizes by pellet brand and signs you're overdoing it.
      • How often to feed goldfish: Twice daily, sinking pellets, with a weekly blanched-pea day to prevent swim-bladder issues. See what to feed goldfish at home for a full breakdown.
      • How much to feed neon tetras: A pinch of micro-pellets or crushed flake twice daily, consumed within 60 seconds — oversized portions are the main cause of neon tetra bloat. Run the numbers for your group size with our neon tetra stocking guide.
      • How much to feed guppies: Small pinch of flake or micro-pellet, twice daily; guppies graze constantly so slight 喂食不足 is safer than 过度喂食.
      • How often to feed cherry shrimp: A tiny pinch every 2–3 days — see our cherry shrimp feeding guide for the full schedule and food list.

      过度喂食的迹象：如何判断喂太多了

      大多数被归咎于疾病的鱼类死亡，实际上源于过度喂食。注意以下迹象：

      • Cloudy or hazy water within hours of feeding — uneaten food and excess waste feeding a bacterial bloom. See our guide on cloudy water causes for the full diagnosis.
      • Rising nitrate or ammonia between water changes, faster than your tank's usual baseline.
      • Bloated, lethargic fish sitting at the bottom, or visible swim-bladder problems in goldfish and bettas.
      • Algae blooms appearing or worsening within days of a feeding change.
      • Food still uneaten after 90 seconds — the simplest tell of all. If you're consistently netting out leftovers, you're feeding too much, full stop.

      如果你的鱼看起来总是很饿——在玻璃前乞食、翻底砂找吃的 — that's a different problem with its own causes; see why your fish always seem hungry before increasing portions.
